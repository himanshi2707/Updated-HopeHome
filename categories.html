DEPRESSION


Depression is a word that is widely used but less understood. Many people struggle with the symptoms of depression without realizing it. You might feel off, as though you are functioning below your normal capacity. Perhaps you struggle to get out of bed in the morning. Maybe you dwell on the tasks you haven’t accomplished and feel irritable and frustrated.

How HopeHome can help to heal your Depression ?

●Healthy coping mechanisms :
Through the course of therapy healthy coping mechanisms and personal self care activities are planned and practiced to cope with stressful situations and prevent relapse.

● Positive, productive thought patterns :
Client is equipped to consciously gain awareness about their unhelpful patterns of thinking and are armed with tools to challenge these thoughts.

● Emotion regulation Techniques :
Are learnt to express and channelize negative emotions in a productive manner thereby increasing positive mood.


Try to get the most out of your online counselling & therapy. Keep your appointments. Practice the exercises your therapist gives you.
Remember, you don’t have to deal with Depression on your own. Help is out here.
